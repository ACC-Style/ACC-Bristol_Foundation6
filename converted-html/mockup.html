Non-statin Agents

If lipid-lowering response is less-than-anticipated (i):

Consider adding ezetimibe first.

Or if ezetimibe-intolerant, and triglycerides >300 mg/dL, may consider a bile acid sequesterant (BAS).

If lipid-lowering response is still less-than-anticipated(i):

Consider adding or replacing current lipid-lowering therapy with PCSK9 inhibitor second

Consider only if patient has been on maximally tolerated statin or is fully statin intolerant, and has persistently insufficient response with ezetimibe orBAS ( i ).


Once acceptable response is achieved with lipid-lowering therapy, continue to monitor LDL-C level and adherence to medications and lifestyle.

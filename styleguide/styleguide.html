Brand Level

var $brand-level- this variable has the ablity to over take the standard acc coloring with a branded product color. Standard values are: 0 - Pure ACC Color; 1 - Header Bar and Primaary Nav Selects; 2 - Lines, Selects, and Header Bar: 3 - Full Color OVer Take, Typography, Selects, Header Bar, and Lines
